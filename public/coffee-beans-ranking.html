Lou's Top V60 Coffee Beans

Discover the magic of brewing with my favorite V60 coffee beans.
Their unique characteristics not only provide a distinct taste but also a journey from different parts of the world right into your coffee cup.
Below are my top 3 picks along with their details and descriptions.

My top 3 beans of all-time

Emirati Coffee - Colombian Blend

Rating: ⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️

Origin: Colombia

Region: Antioquia

Farm: Finca La Loma

Process: Carb Maceration

Variety: Castillo, Caturra

Description:

This exquisite blend hails from the verdant region of Antioquia.
The unique Carb Maceration process unfolds a spectrum of flavors that are as rich and deep as the Colombian soil.
The blend of Castillo and Caturra beans brings a balanced cup, with a robust body and a flavor profile that dances between fruity and nutty, perfect for those who appreciate complexity in their brew.

Emirati Coffee - Antioquian Delight

Rating: ⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️

Origin: Colombia

Region: Antioquia

Farm: Finca Las Mercedes

Process: Natural

Variety: Caturra

Description:

From the heart of Colombia's coffee belt, this blend offers a different take on the region's coffee.
The Natural process allows the bean to retain its inherent sweetness, mirroring the lush landscapes of the Antioquia region.
The Caturra variety brings a light, airy quality to the cup, making it a delightful choice for the everyday coffee lover.

Emirati Coffee - Ethiopian Yirgacheffe

Rating: ⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️

Origin: Ethiopia

Varietal: Mostly Kurume

Farm: Finca La Loma

Process: Natural

Description:

TJourney to the heart of Ethiopia with this Yirgacheffe coffee.
The predominantly Kurume varietal unveils a captivating cup profile of strawberry candy floss, stewed apricot, and dark toffee.
Its full body and creamy mouthfeel paired with a delicate acidity make it a divine choice for both filter coffee and espresso.
The high-rating is a testimony to its exceptional quality and enchanting flavors that are reminiscent of Ethiopian heritage.
